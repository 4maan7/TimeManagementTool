Project Role

As a group leader my main role is to organise group meetings which are a useful tool as it gives an opportunity for each member in the group to brainstorm ideas and share any solutions they may have. Moreover, as the group leader I also complete the documentation on behalf of the group. This includes meeting notes on any work or discussion we may have, also includes screenshots of evidence of work the group completed alongside a short explanation on how we performed the task. In addition, communication is key and as group leader I ensure that everyone knows what their role is and meet deadlines. If there are any questions I can relay the queries to necessary team member.
